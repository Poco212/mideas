# metadata for book

## Title
The name of the book. example : laskar pelangi 

## Creator
A person who has an access of the book. example: andrea hirata

## Subject 
Topics discussed

## Description
An explanation of the content of the resource, such as an abstract or summary.

## Publisher
The person responsible for providing resources

## Contributor 
Parties who contribute to the content, but are not the primary creators.

## Year 
The year the book was published 

## Type
The physical or digital form. Example: file format such as PDF

## Identifier 
A unique reference to the resource. Example: ISBN.

## Language 
The language used in the resource's content.

## License
Information about copyright or rights management

## Book Cover
Cover of the book

## Edition
Related edition of the book
